For a detailed study of Ergodic theory, I will return when I systematically review measure theoretic probability theory.

A $\sigma$ ring of $X$ is a collection of subsets of $X$ that is closed under countable union and difference. 

A map $T$ of $X$ into $X$ is called $S$-measurable  if $T^{-1}E\in S$ for all $E\in S$.
