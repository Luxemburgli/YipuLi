 Hi! I'm **Yipu Li** (李一朴) and I go by the English name **Lux**.
 
 ![[Yipu Li.jpg|200]]

I'm currently a master student at [Institution of Logic, Language and Computation](https://www.illc.uva.nl/) at University of Amsterdam. I'm a [MSc Logic](https://msclogic.illc.uva.nl/programme/mol/) student following the logic and mathematics track. I obtained my bachelor at [Peking University ](https://english.pku.edu.cn/).

My academic interest lies in applying logic to artificial intelligence to make it more explainable, trustworthy and in general more aligned with human reasoning. The following page introduces my work on formal systems that represents human knowledge:[[Logic systems that models human knowledge systems]] . I'm doing a project under the supervision of Dr. Fausto Carcassi on [ARC-AGI](https://arcprize.org/) benchmark, which is a benchmark that test the model's generalizing ability and efficiency in acquiring new skills. This is our repo: [LIPS-ARC-Project](https://github.com/dt-lindberg/LIPS-ARC-Project).

I'm also working a lot on set theory, my central interest lies in descriptive set theory and the independent results revolving around the reals. I'm especially drawn to how and why set theoretic assumptions have an effect on the regularity of the reals. This page tracks my studies in set theory: [[Set Theory Index]].

Aside from these, I have great amateur interests in Chinese classics, ancient and modern thoughts in China and dramas. Do check this ([[On Classical Chinese Ideals]]) out if you are interested in in my take on Chinese Classics. I'm a cycler, tennis player, chess player and a growing cook in my life.
