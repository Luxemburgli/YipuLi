In 2024, together with Tenyo Takahashi, I did a project under the supervision of Dr. Yurii Khomski on Iterated forcing and Solovay model. In the project, we worked on the basic theory of iterated forcing, Martin's Axiom, random and Cohen reals and Solovay's Model, following chiefly Jech and Kunen. Here is the report and notes I produced for the project: [[Note_for_the_forcing_project (1).pdf]].

In early 2025, I did a research oriented project under the supervision of Dr. Yurii Khomski on Ramsey sets and Mad family, a rapidly evolving topic in the intersection of descriptive set theory and combinatorial set theory. The materials covered involves the basics of the theory of Ramset sets and Ellentuck space (Equivalent to the relevant section in Jech Ch.26), and some papers by David Schrittesser and Asger TÅNornquist. See the report for details: [[Report for the project on Ramsey property and MAD families.pdf]].

Surprisingly, I found myself not far away from understanding Martin-Steel theorem if I follow Ralf Schindler's text book. Hence I decided to devote myself studying the book and I aim to provide a detailed exercise solution for the exercises in Ch. 9 to 13.
